.. _admin_roundcube-settings_flag_for_deletion:

``flag_for_deletion``
=====================

When a user deletes a message, should Roundcube flag the message as
deleted in IMAP, or move the message to the Trash folder?

The default in Kolab Groupware is to flag a message as deleted.
